How long does a water filter last?

A hollow fibre hiking filter lasts roughly 1,500 litres in clean water, or about one season of weekend use.

What shortens filter life?

Silt is the main culprit. Sediment clogs the membrane far faster than clear water.

How do you extend it?

  • Backflush after every trip
  • Pre-filter silty water
  • Store fully dry
  • Never let it freeze

When should you replace it?

Replace when backflushing no longer restores flow.
